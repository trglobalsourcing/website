About TR Global

Connecting global buyers with Sri Lanka’s manufacturing excellence.

TR Global Solutions (Pvt) Ltd. is a Sri Lanka-based import and export solutions company focused on sourcing, manufacturing coordination and global trade management.

Positioning

A practical sourcing partner for buyers who need reliable production, clear communication and organized export support.

Not just a supplier list.

International sourcing only works when the small details are managed properly. Product specifications, production capability, timeline expectations, packaging, inspection and documentation all need to connect before a shipment leaves the factory.

TR Global works as the coordination point between buyers and selected manufacturing partners, helping turn product requirements into organized sourcing plans.
